description=## Features
description=
description=According to actual katana facts, this mod adds Michonne's Katana based on the following stats
description=
description=- A real katana weight is around 1.1-1.5 kg. This mod makes it 1.5 kg. instead of 2.
description=- A real katana blade length is around 80cm. This mod slightly increase from 0.4 to 0.5 tile and increase reach from 1.4 to 1.5 tiles.
description=- Really, it shouldn't break so easily. The max condition is adjusted to 100 instead of 10.
description=- Lower chance to decrease katana's condition
description=- Allow attaching a katana on a belt
description=- Critical chance and damage is significantly increased.
description=
description=This mod preserves the Base Katana stats. The changes above are only applied to Michonne's Katana.
description=
description=How to Forge Katana magazines spawn at Bookstore, Classroom, Crate, Engineer Tools, Library, Living Room, Post Office, Shelf, Tool Store. Bookstore, Tool Store and Engineer Tools have the highest chance.
description=
description=Michonne's Katana has the same spawning rate as normal Katana.
description=
description=## How to forge a katana
description=
description=- You will need Metalworking Level 6
description=- Read How to Forge Katana magazine
description=- Find tools; Welder Mask, Propane Torch, and Sledgehammer
description=- Find materials; 10 Metal Sheet, 2 Small Metal Sheet, 4 Welding Rods, 1 Leather Strip, 1 Stake, and 1 Wood Glue
description=- [B41] Right-click on any material and click Forge Katana
description=- [B42] Open Crafting Menu, looks for Forge Katana
